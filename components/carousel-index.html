Carousel Components

Two separate implementations for different use cases. Choose the one that fits your needs.

üìÑ Pagination Mode

Slide-by-slide navigation with full user control. Perfect for interactive browsing.

  • Previous/Next buttons
  • Dot indicators (dynamic)
  • Keyboard navigation
  • Touch swipe support
  • Auto-advance (4 seconds)
  • Pause on hover

Best for:

  • Service offerings
  • Team profiles
  • Customer testimonials
  • Product features
  • Case studies

üîÑ Continuous Scroll Mode

Infinite smooth scrolling without interruption. Ideal for ambient displays.

  • Infinite loop scrolling
  • No buttons/indicators
  • Smooth constant motion
  • Pause on hover
  • Auto card duplication
  • Lightweight & clean

Best for:

  • Partner/sponsor logos
  • Certifications/awards
  • Recent projects
  • Stats/achievements
  • Social media feed
